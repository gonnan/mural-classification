Mural Classification

The intention of this web app is to take any image of a mural from Mural Festival, and say who painted it, along with other details about the artist. Currently the model is only trained on existing murals painted for Mural Festival, with a few non festival pieces. I plan on adding to this as I go, but as is, it has about a 99% accuracy on catalogued murals.
